Our Mission

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of sustainability enthusiasts, Thrift Sathi began as a small online community dedicated to sharing and trading pre-owned treasures. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a vibrant marketplace, driven by the belief that fashion can be both stylish and eco-conscious. We're proud to offer a curated selection that helps our customers express their individuality while contributing to a healthier planet.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Prioritizing environmental responsibility in every aspect of our operations.
  • Community: Fostering a supportive and engaging platform for thrift lovers.
  • Quality: Ensuring all items meet our standards for condition and appeal.
  • Affordability: Making unique, stylish finds accessible to a wider audience.
  • Transparency: Being open about our processes and impact.
